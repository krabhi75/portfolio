Who I Am

ABOUT
ME.

A product builder who turns ambiguity into clarity, ships with conviction, and grows products with data as the compass.

I am a Product Management professional currently working as an Assistant Product Manager in the FinTech lending space, with hands-on experience building and scaling products across the end-to-end gold loan lifecycle.

In my current role at Capri Global Capital, I own and improve critical product workflows spanning customer onboarding, gold valuation, credit checks, disbursal, renewals, and loan closure. I work at the intersection of business, technology, and compliance — collaborating closely with credit, risk, operations, engineering, and partner banks to deliver scalable and compliant lending solutions.

A key part of my work involves driving co-lending product initiatives with partner banks, ensuring seamless coordination across stakeholders while building systems that are both operationally efficient and regulatorily aligned.
