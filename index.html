Who I Am

ABOUT
ME

A product thinker who builds with clarity, ships with confidence, and grows with data.

I'm a Product Manager with a track record of building 0-to-1 products and scaling them to significant user bases. My approach blends deep user empathy with structured frameworks — I never build solutions looking for problems.

Whether it's a B2B SaaS platform, a consumer app, or an internal tool, I bring structure to chaos and clarity to ambiguity. I work closely with design, engineering, and business stakeholders to ship products that actually move the needle.

Currently open to senior PM roles in high-growth startups and product-led organizations.
