Who I Am

ABOUT
ME.

A product builder who turns ambiguity into clarity, ships with conviction, and grows products with data as the compass.

I'm a Product Manager with a proven track record of building products from 0 to 1 and scaling them to significant user bases. My approach marries deep user empathy with structured frameworks — I never build solutions in search of problems.

Whether it's a B2B SaaS platform for the Indian gold loan market, an AI-powered approval mechanism, or a consumer-facing job discovery engine — I bring structure to chaos and turn complexity into elegant product decisions.

I work in the sweet spot where business goals, user needs, and engineering reality intersect. Currently open to senior PM roles at high-growth, product-led organizations.
